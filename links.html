About The Project

This immersive digital experience celebrates the timeless poetry of Li Bai (李白), the immortal poet of the Tang Dynasty. The project combines elegant modern web design with traditional Chinese aesthetics to create an interactive journey through his most famous works. Featuring responsive design, smooth animations, and bilingual presentations, this website serves as both an educational resource and an artistic tribute to China's poetic heritage.
